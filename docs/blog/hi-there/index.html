About me

Hi there!

Jesús David Díaz González here, a software developer from La Aldea de San Nicolás[1][2].

After working for the Web platform during 10 years, recently I became a YaST Team Member (YTM) 👏👏👏 at SUSE, a great and exciting challenge which allows me to leave the comfort zone and dedicate my work-time to an open source project.

I have always wanted to have a blog to tell something, but until now… nevermind. I will try to do it from now on, although I am not quite sure if I will have enough discipline 😅, motivation 💪 and, above all, (interesting) things to write 😎

This first entry can be considered as a mix between the classic Hello World! and a partial About me section.

Let’s go.
